Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data at any time. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.

Do you offer discounts for multiple properties?

Enterprise plans include unlimited properties. For multiple Pro accounts, contact our sales team for volume discounts.

Can I use BuckScience offline?

BuckScience is a web-based platform requiring internet access. However, you can export data for offline analysis and review.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for any plan. You only pay the monthly or annual subscription fee. Enterprise customers may have custom setup for integrations.
