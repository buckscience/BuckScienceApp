Our Mission

To empower hunters, land managers, and wildlife researchers with data-driven insights that improve hunting success while promoting ethical and sustainable hunting practices.

BuckScience bridges the gap between traditional hunting wisdom and modern technology, providing tools that help hunters understand deer behavior patterns, optimize property management, and make informed decisions based on real data rather than guesswork.

Our Story

BuckScience was born from the frustration of sifting through thousands of trail camera photos without clear insights.

Our founders, experienced hunters and technology professionals, recognized that the hunting community was sitting on a goldmine of data but lacked the tools to extract meaningful patterns. Trail cameras were generating thousands of photos, but hunters were still relying on intuition and limited manual analysis.

We saw an opportunity to apply modern data science, machine learning, and spatial analysis to hunting data, creating a platform that could reveal patterns invisible to the human eye and predict deer movement with unprecedented accuracy.
