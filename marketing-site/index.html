About BuckScience

BuckScience was born from the passion of hunters who wanted to apply modern data science to the age-old pursuit of understanding deer behavior.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with practical hunting wisdom to help hunters, land managers, and wildlife researchers make data-driven decisions. From weekend hunters to professional wildlife managers, BuckScience provides the tools you need to understand deer patterns and optimize your strategy.

Built with Clean Architecture principles and powered by advanced spatial analytics, BuckScience transforms raw hunting data into actionable intelligence that improves success rates and land management decisions.
